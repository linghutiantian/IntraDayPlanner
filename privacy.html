Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Catlendar ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our day planning service.

Information We Collect

  • Local Storage Data: We store your planner data locally on your device using browser local storage.
  • Google Calendar Data: If you choose to import events from Google Calendar, we request access to your calendar data. This data is only used for display within the application and is not stored on our servers.
  • Usage Preferences: We store your app preferences (such as dark mode, time range settings, and density preferences) locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To save your planned events and preferences
  • To display your Google Calendar events when you choose to import them

Data Storage

All of your planner data and preferences are stored locally on your device using browser local storage. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device
  • We do not transmit or store your planner data on our servers
  • Your data may be lost if you clear your browser cache or local storage

Google Calendar Integration

Our application offers integration with Google Calendar. When you choose to use this feature:

  • You will be asked to grant permission for our application to access your Google Calendar data
  • We only access the calendar data you specifically request to import
  • We do not store your Google Calendar data on our servers
  • The connection to Google Calendar is governed by Google's Privacy Policy

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies to collect information about you or your activities.

Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Calendar API: Used to import your calendar events when you choose to do so. Google's use of your data is governed by their Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@catlendar.online.
